Adopted Hosts

Chez fur has been a longtime partner of the ASPCA. They are the primary source of our hosts.
We take in some of their cats in order to make room for other kitties in need while they help us rehome.
All of our hosts are available for adoption. You may find that one of our cudly kitties would like to adopt you.

If you feel that the relationship is strong between you and one of our hosts dont hesitate to ask our staff about the adoption process! ! !
